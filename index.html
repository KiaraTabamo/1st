Banana bread is high in antioxidants

which help to support cellular health and reduce harmful free radicals.

Free radicals are produced when your body breaks down food or when you are exposed to cigarette smoke or radiation.

Banana bread is a source of protein

Banana bread can be high in carbohydrates but it can also be a good source of protein.

Protein is essential for muscle growth and repair and everyone needs this in their diet. You may need more protein if you work out or if you are training for a sporting event.

Banana bread is high in magnesium

Bananas are also high in magnesium. Magnesium is involved in a number of processes in the body, including regulating the nervous system and increasing energy.

Magnesium can also help to improve your performance when you are exercising or taking part in sports.
